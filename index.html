Work-Life Balance

The Key to Personal and Professional Success

  • Lower work-life balance associated with worse mental and physical health in Warsaw study. (Source)
  • Work-life balance positively influences job satisfaction in Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). (Source)
  • Healthy work-life balance increases job satisfaction and organizational commitment. (Source)
  • Burnout, exacerbated by poor work-life balance, significantly reduces productivity. (Source)
  • Work-life balance programs significantly impact employee engagement, which in turn increases productivity and profitability. (Source)

The Five Balls of Life

"Imagine life as a game in which you are juggling some five balls in the air. You name them work, family, health, friends and spirit. And you're keeping all of these in the air. You will soon understand that work is a rubber ball. If you drop it, it will bounce back. But the other four balls – family, health, friends and spirit – are made of glass. If you drop one of these, they will be irrevocably scuffed, marked, nicked, damaged or even shattered. They will never be the same. You must understand that and strive for balance in your life." - Brian Dyson, former Coca-Cola CEO
